Site Plan

Site Name

Clean Sweep

This name represents a feeling of cleanliness for my cleaning website. its also a relatively known phrase.

Site Purpose

Clean Sweep is a cleaning service website designed to support a future small business by providing a user-friendly platform for potential customers. The site will showcase before-and-after galleries, introduce the company and its team, and offer transparent pricing for various services including home, room, garage, and move-in/move-out cleanings. It will also feature a quote calculator to help users estimate costs and a community-driven blog where people can share cleaning tips and tricks. This project serves as a practical foundation for launching a real-world cleaning business, inspired by a family memberâ€™s entrepreneurial goals.
